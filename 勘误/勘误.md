$$
L_\delta=\left\{
\begin{array}{rcl}
\frac{1}{2}(y_i-f(x_i))     &      & {\mid y_i-f(x_i)\mid < \delta }\\
\delta (y_i-f(x_i)) - \frac{1}{2}\delta   &      & {其他}\\
\end{array} \right. 
$$

$$
L_\delta=\left\{
\begin{array}{rcl}

\delta \ (\mid y_i-f(x_i)\mid - \frac{1}{2}{\delta})   &      & {其他}\\
\end{array} \right.
$$
